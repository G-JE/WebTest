Engineering Skills

Programming
I have taken courses in C++ and am currently studying C for embedded computer systems. I have indepedently studied HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, Node.js and some python.
Software
I am SolidWorks Certified. I have taken courses in MATlab, R Studio, MultiSim, PSpice, LabVIEW. I worked professionally with AutoCAD. I have also used the Adobe Suite for a variety of design projects. I am also very skilled in using Microsoft Office.
Engineering Tools
Through my work in laboratories and industry I have been exposed to and used a variety of tools. For Electrical I have used Digital Multimeters, Oscilloscopes, Breakbaords for analog circuitry, and microcontrollers for digital design. For Mechanical I have used the Instron Strength Testing Machine (Both Torque and Tensile), drill presses, power sanders, and variety of power tools.

Projects

Electrical Projects
During my time at Benjamin Franklin Institute of Technology I built an entire residential 100A main electrical box. The box supplied 3 15A general branch circuits, a dryer unit and a fridgerator unit on GFCI protected branch. The box was metered and was fed by a 1/0 Aluminum wire through weather head. At Wentworth I have done many circuit analysis labs ranging from AC-DC converted ciruit to an AB transistor amplifier circuit for analog. For digital I have created a timer using 8 bar LED numeric and a controller.
Mechanical Projects
Designed and innovated the medical crutch for improved user experience through reduction of under arm pressure and scalability for any sized patient. Model was tested with SolidWorks FEA for maximized carrying capacity. this.
Coding Projects
I coded and designed a pacman game that is run using C++, I utilized Simlple Direct Media Layer as the library for the visualization of the game. A repo of the game can be seen here. This website is another coding project of mine.
